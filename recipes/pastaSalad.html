Pasta Salad

Descrition

An amazing recipe to create the perfect side dish to your meal!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz package fusili pasta
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 pound provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 pound salama, cubed
  • 1/4 pound sliced pepperoni, halved
  • 1 large green bell pepper, in 1 inch pieces
  • 10 oz canned black olives
  • 4 oz primentos jar
  • 8 oz italian salad dressing bottle

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of slightly salted water, add the pasta in it, stirring occasionally for about 12 minutes, then drain.
  2. Put the pasta and all the other ingredients into a large bowl. Pour the salad dressing then toss around to coat it.
  3. Enjoy!
